Lions And Eagles 2020

The Lions and Eagles Gathering is an Apostolic/Prophetic Gathering of Men and Women who carry “breakthrough” in their spiritual DNA. This Gathering is strategic in nature for the advancement of the Kingdom Of God in lives of all who attend. The Lion’s and Eagle’s will gather for worship, encounter, and strategy that will launch all those who attend back into their everyday life with the tools they need to impact the world/sphere they are called to influence. We believe we are called to be the change that will bring reform to our culture. We are answering that call. JOIN US OCTOBER 15-18
