Our Story

What started out as a humble little Church coming out of The Southern Baptist Denomination, Has been taking New Leaps and Bounds in pursuing the Calling and Commissioning of God in The Natural. It all began in the mid 70’s- In the Middle of The Charismatic Movement. We were Challenged by the Lord to go beyond the boundaries and norm of what we used to Know. There was an outpouring of His Spirit, some were enjoying it and wanted to stay and continue with us, others moved on. Still we chose to go on with God !.

  • In 1982 a Couple Named Jack and Jean Oden came alongside the Church and began to build on that foundation- Jack bringing The Prophetic and Jean bringing Teaching, Our Church was catapulted into world of flowing in the Gifts of The Spirit and and a solid Understanding to go with it.
  • After the passing of Beloved Pastor Jack, God began giving Jean Oden a mandate to Pursue The Fullness of the Glory God,and a Greater Hunger for Worship and Sound- We had several years of simply exploring Gods Dreams for us, and we encountered many hard times. We still, even though we had many obstacles, we could never forget God’s Dream.We had been given many Prophetic Words about Our Destiny.
  • In 2016, The Lord begins to Speak to Jean Oden that Our Church again, needs to shift into the New! She shares that God was wanting us to Shift into an Apostolic, Five Fold Ministry Type Body of People. We began to explore that Reality and all that means- It wasn’t long into Our Transition when Pastor Jean Passed on to be with the Lord in September of 2017.
  • After the Loss of our Beloved Leader, we still Couldn’t forget God’s Dream. Today we are moving towards and becoming an Apostolic Center- We are still at our core, a Prophetic People with Strong Teaching and Wisdom- But Now God has given us an Apostolic Cause in the Earth! Were hoping that You will Be a Part of it!
